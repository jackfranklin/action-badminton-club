The Club

About Us

We are a friendly and competitive club based in Ealing. Our club nights run on Tuesdays from 8-10pm, across four courts and feather shuttles are used.

Our club nights are used to catch up with members, meet guests and practice for upcoming matches. Following training, there is normally a visit to the local pub.

We are currently looking for league standard men and ladies to strengthen our teams, so sadly, we cannot accept beginners.

Location

We play at the Twyford Sports Centre (W3 9PP), near the heart of Acton.

We are well located being less than 10 minutes’ walk from both Acton Town (Piccadilly) and Ealing Common (District).

If you prefer to bus, then the 207, 427,440, 607 and E3 all pass right by. There is also ample space for cars and bicycles on site.

The venue is a fairly new sports hall with good lighting, 4 courts and plenty of changing facilities. There is also room off court for bags and benches for seating between games.

Our matches are played at The Dome, the home of Middlesex County Badminton, in Hounslow (UB2 5TZ).
