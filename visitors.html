Visitors

We aim to always make guests feel welcome and give them a chance to meet the committee, as well as playing a mixture of games throughout the evening.

All visitors are asked to contact us and to have received a reply prior to turning up to club nights in order to ensure that the courts do not become too busy.

Please be aware that guests who do not confirm their visit with the committee maybe be turned away if the evening is busy.

We currently have limited spaces and so are looking to get ladies and men who would be looking to develop our teams

If you are interested in visiting the club, please do not hesitate to contact us at: info@actonbc.co.uk
